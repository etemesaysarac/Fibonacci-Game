About the Fibonacci Sequence

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1. It was introduced to Western mathematics by Leonardo of Pisa, known as Fibonacci, in his 1202 book *Liber Abaci*. The ratio of successive Fibonacci numbers approximates the golden ratio, which appears in nature, art, and architecture.
